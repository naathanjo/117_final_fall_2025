# Legal & Compliance Checklist

Cover the basics of professional web standards.

- [ ] **Copyright Notice**: Include a copyright year and your name/brand in the footer (e.g., "Â© 2025 Your Name").
- [ ] **Privacy Policy**: Since you are collecting data (contact forms, analytics), include a link to a basic Privacy Policy in the footer.
- [ ] **Cookie Consent**: If your analytics or tools use cookies, display a simple banner notifying the user.
- [ ] **Asset Attribution**: If you used free stock photos or icons that require attribution, include a credits page or mention them in the footer/code comments.
- [ ] **GDPR/CCPA**: Ensure your contact form includes a checkbox for users to agree to be contacted.
