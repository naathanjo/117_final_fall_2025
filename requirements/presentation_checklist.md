# Final Presentation Checklist

Your 5-minute pitch deck is your chance to sell yourself as an AI Product Engineer.

## Presentation Structure
- [ ] **The Hook**: Start with a compelling opening that defines your value proposition.
- [ ] **Case Study 1 (Portfolio)**: Showcase your personal brand, archetype, and persuasion strategy.
- [ ] **Case Study 2 (Client Work)**: Demonstrate how you solved a problem for a client (GreenLeaf Landscaping or similar).
- [ ] **Technical Mastery**: Briefly highlight your CI/CD pipeline and quality gates (Lighthouse scores).
- [ ] **Key Learnings**: A dedicated slide summarizing your top takeaways from the IS117 course.
- [ ] **AI Collaboration Process**: A dedicated slide analyzing your workflow with AI.
    - [ ] Include insights from asking the AI: *"Describe our collaboration and my prompting strategy"*
    - [ ] Include insights from asking the AI: *"How can I improve my collaboration and prompting strategy?"*
- [ ] **Call to Action**: End with a clear next step for the client (e.g., "Let's schedule a consultation").

## Delivery
- [ ] **Time Management**: Ensure the presentation fits strictly within 5 minutes.
- [ ] **Visuals**: Use high-quality visuals (Canva is recommended). Avoid walls of text.
- [ ] **Professionalism**: Present as if you are in a real business meeting.
